Introduction

Listening Is an essential and challenging topic for the majority of language learners.Teaching listening for new students is also challenging for teachers.When teaching Levantine Arabic you have to teach culture, food traditions and beliefs. In this training, we are trying to shed light on the importance of implementing Multimedia principles in the listening material and present authentic listening with pictures. To do so we are integrating authentic listening with principles through Camtasia.

You will be navigating through the following topics:
