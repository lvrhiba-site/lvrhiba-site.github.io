Step by step job aid

Adding Vocabulary by implementing, Multimedia, Contiguity and Signaling Principle.Make your video interactive with Camtasia

Step 1: Click on Annotation.

First click on Annotation.
As you can see nothing displayed between the canvas and the tool bar.You will notice the difference when you click on annotation in step 2.

Step 2: Choose callouts.

After you click on Annotations a collection of Callouts is shown.
You can pick any style you want.We will be using them to write vocabulary and placing the vocabulary above the picture that corresponds to it.

Step 3: Drag and Drop callouts.

Pick one of the callouts' shapes.
Drag and drop the callouts to track 2 above the screencast video. Make sure to place it above the exact segment of your listening ineterval .

You can also change the size of each box as per your text need.

Step 4: Activate the Quiz button.

Click on the small "v" under the + sign. Once you click on it, you will be able to add a quiz to the timeline curser.

Step 5: Quiz instruction.

Notice how the Quiz option popped out after clicking on the v sign.
Also, you can see a long transparent row alongside to word Quiz. You have to click on this transparent to create a new Quiz. After you finish with your quizzes click the arrow again to deactivate the quiz option.

Step 6: Placing quiz question.

Now after you have the quiz button on, just click on the timeline curser to add a quiz, or click above the media where you want to place it.
Add your question.Once you click on the quiz sign you will see a message Quiz Placeholder at the middle of the Canvas telling you that your quiz will be placed here.

Step 7: Questions and Feedback.

After you click on the Question button, you will see the questions bar to the right side of the canvas. Choose the type of questions you want.
After you write your question, click on the empty box next to Display Feedback. Place a checkmark there to activate the feedback box.

Step 8: Exporting File.

When you click on the green Export button, a list of options will appear in a dropdown menu.

Most of the options are prepaid, the ones we want for our project are:

  • Export to local file:allows you to have your project saved on the shared drive.
  • Export to YouTube:allows you to have your own YouTube video, either a screencast or any other video you create.

Step 9:Exporting Quiz.

Once you click Export to Local File, the following table will be asking you how you want to save and export your file. Name your file and save it in a folder on Desctop or Document.

Here are a few steps you have to double-check before clicking on the final Export button:

  • For file Format, check Export to MP4.
  • Check next to Export as Web Page.
  • Check next to Include Quiz.
