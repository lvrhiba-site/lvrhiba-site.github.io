  • Post-test

Task 1:Screencast

Create a screencast instructional video. The video time limit is no more than 20- 30 seconds. You can use the topic you want to instruct your colleagues about. For example, it could be about how to open a word document? How to go to my folder on share drive? How to access the curriculum on Teams?.

Task 2: Create an Interactive video

Use Camtasia software to create your own interactive video. The video should be implementing Multimedia, Contiguity, and Signaling principles with vocabulary in context. Make sure to include at least three quizzes with feedback. Post your video on the share drive. .
