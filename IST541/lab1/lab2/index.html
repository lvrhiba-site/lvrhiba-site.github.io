How to Use Callouts on Camtasia

By Hiba Elrayes


Camtasia, is a straight forward software,reliable and functional,it has proven its effecacy in creating screencasts and interactive materials.In these five steps you will be learning the basics. For more instruction watch the Demo link at the end on this lesson.

Step 1: Click on Annotation.

You should have your Camtasia software downloaded on your computer.

First step, open Camtasia, and then start with importing a picture from your computer desktop or Library, then click on Annotation.

Step 2: Callouts.

After you click on Annotation, you will directly see the Callouts menu, you can navigate through many shapes and colors.

In the Style tab, there are four diffrent styles:

  • Abstract
  • Basic
  • Bold
  • Industrial

Step 3: Drag and Drop.

Pick one of the callouts' shapes, drag it, and drop it to the timeline on Track 3.

You can also change the size of each box as per your text need.


Step 4: Write inside the Box.

Double click inside the callouts, a cursor will show up, write the word “House”, or any other word that serves your topic.


Step 5: Change Background Color.

Click on the color drop, to the right and change the Callouts' background color.
