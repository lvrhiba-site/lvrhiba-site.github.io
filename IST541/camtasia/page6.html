Step by step job aid

Adding Vocabulary by implementing Contiguity and Signaling Principle, making your video interactive with Camtasia

Step 1: Click on Annotation.

First click on Annotation.
As you can see nothing displayed between the canvas and the tool bar, you will notice the difference when you click on annotation in step 2.

Step 2: Choose callouts.

After you click on Annotations a collection of Callouts showed.
You can pick any style you want.We will be using them to write vocabulary and place it above the picture that corresponds to it

Step 3: Drag and Drop callouts.

Pick one of the callouts' shapes.
Drag and drop the callouts to track 2 above the screencast video, make sure to place it above the exact segment of your listening ineterval .

You can also change the size of each box as per your text need.

Step 4: Activate the Quiz button.

Click on the small + sign. Once you click on it, you will be able to add quiz to the timeline curser.

Step 5: Quiz instruction.

Notice how the Quiz option popped out after clicking on the + sign.
Click the + sign again if you want to deactivate the quiz option.

Step 6: Placing quiz question.

Now after you have the quiz button on, just click on the timeline curser to add a quiz, or click above the media where you want to place it.
Add your question.Once you click on the quiz sign you will see a message Quiz Placeholder at the middle of the Canvas, telling you that your quiz will be placed here.

Step 7: Questions and Feedback.

After you click on the Question button, you will see the questions bar to the right side of the canvas.Choose the type of questions you want. After you write your question click on the empty box next to Display Feedback, place a checkmark there to activate the feedback box.
